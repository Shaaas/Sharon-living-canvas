My Work

I create in two worlds. As a writer, I explore love, growing up, grief, and the small absurdities of being human. My essays and poems are honest reflections on living — not advice, just observations from someone living through it.

As a developer, I build interactive experiences that blur the line between code and art. I'm interested in how technology can tell stories, create presence, and make people feel something.
